Overview

Purpose

The purpose of a rafting waters website is to inform potential customers about the business's rafting experience, pricing, safety measures, and reviews. It helps attract new customers and build brand awareness.

Audience

Who are the target customers?

Our target customers are young adventure seekers who have disposable income and are looking for an adrenaline-filled experience. They are interested in outdoor activities and have a passion for exploring new things.

What do they want?

They want an exciting and challenging rafting experience that will push them out of their comfort zone and create lasting memories.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

They expect to find information on the different types of rafting experiences we offer, including the difficulty level and length of each trip. They also expect to find information on our safety measures and guides, as well as reviews and testimonials from past customers.
