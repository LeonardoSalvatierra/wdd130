RIVER RUNNERS

JOIN US ON THE GREATEST ADVENTURE OF YOUR LIFE

Embark on a thrilling adventure as we conquer the rapids of the Atuel River, flowing through the breathtaking canyons of Valle Grande. Our skilled guides will navigate you through Class III and IV rapids, offering an adrenaline-filled experience amidst the stunning landscapes of Mendoza.

ATUEL RIVER - MENDOZA

Join us on an unforgettable journey down the mighty Río Juramento in Salta, renowned for its challenging Class V rapids. Prepare to be mesmerized by the dramatic scenery of Quebrada de las Conchas as you navigate through towering cliffs and rugged canyons, immersing yourself in the natural wonders of Northwestern Argentina

JURAMENTO RIVER - SALTA

Experience a scenic two-day rafting trip on the picturesque Río Manso, nestled in the heart of the Nahuel Huapi National Park in Bariloche. Challenge yourself with Class II and III rapids while surrounded by stunning mountain landscapes, lush forests, and the pristine beauty of Patagonia.

MANSO RIVER (NAHUEL HUAPI) - BARILOCHE

Embark on an epic three-day rafting expedition along the legendary Río Paraná in the Iguazu region. Brace yourself for the thrill of conquering Class III and IV rapids while marveling at the impressive Iguazu Falls, exploring hidden river channels, and camping in remote spots along the riverbanks.

PARANÁ RIVER - IGUAZU MISIONES

Indulge in a family-friendly white water rafting adventure on the scenic Río Calchaquí, flowing through the picturesque landscapes of Cafayate in the province of Salta. Enjoy a day of Class I and II rapids, surrounded by stunning vineyards, towering red rock formations, and the enchanting charm of Northwest Argentina.
